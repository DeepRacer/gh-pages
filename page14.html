DeepRacing is a group of
Silicon Valley deep learning wizards & hardware hackers, trying to push the autonomous robot/vehicle revolution, closer to reality - one hack at-a-time...

Having started as a meetup group, DeepRacing is evolving into a unique Robotics & A.I. playground. Sponsored by some of the major core-technology companies. We will soon amass the technology and equipment to be en par.


Located in South San Francisco Bay, we aim to optimize towards performance and cost.  Effectively riding the wave of innovation that is coming from the 1/10th-1/16th scale autonomous vehicles (i.e. hacked remote control cars).  This can be amazing fun, and useful for those wanting to dive into machine vision, machine learning, deep learning, autonomous vehicles, and in general *robotics*
